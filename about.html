About Us

Hello everyone, and welcome to the launch of HSBG Club! HSBG Club is a platform
designed to host weekly tournaments with cash prizes.

By holding multiple tournaments throughout the week at various times, there will
be many opportunities for players from all over the world to compete. We want to
provide a fun and safe environment for people who love playing Hearthstone
Battlegrounds as much as we do. Your first week of membership in the club will be
free. We hope to see you there!
