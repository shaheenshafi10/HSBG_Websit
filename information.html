Format

16 player Bracket
Each tournament will consist of 16 players with 2 rounds. The 16 players will be
divided into two lobbies. Each round will consist of 3 games. The top 4 players of
each lobby will advance to round 2.

32 Player Bracket

Each tournament will consist of 32 players with 3 rounds. The 32 players will be
divided into 4 initial lobbies. Each round will consist of 3 games, and the top 4
players of each lobby will advance to round 2.

Points and Prizes Distributions

The point system is the same for both 16-person and 32-person tournaments.
Points are split evenly in the case of a tie.

Prizes are determined by the total prize pool based on the number of entries.
The fixed rate, for 16-person tournaments, is a static 70% of the prize pool for
1st, and 30% for second. For 32-person tournaments, 1st place obtains 40% of
the prize pool. 2nd wins 30%, and 3rd and 4th earn 20% and 10% of the total
respectively.

Check in will be open 2 hours before the tournament. Please Check in if you are
even on the wait list as the system will automatically replace all players that have
not checked in.
